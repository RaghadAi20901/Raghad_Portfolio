Who I Am

Hi, I'm Raghad, Computer Science student from Saudi Arabia.

I analyze details so deeply that even the bugs in my code start to feel seen and understood before I delete them from existence. This obsession turned into a skill called attention to detail useful for coding, dangerous for overthinking. My mind enjoys AI, finding patterns, and exposing hidden points like a detective who never sleeps and definitely needs therapy. I love learning from different fields, collecting knowledge like a dragon hoards treasure — except my treasure is information, not gold, and my cave is GitHub.

When I plan something, I use the divide-and-conquer method because breaking problems into pieces is more satisfying than breaking people (usually). I always aim to finish my work perfectly and efficiently… or die trying. (Still undecided.)
